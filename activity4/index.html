Uses the Time Machine to transport Nobita and his friends across time and space to solve planetary crises and save magical worlds.

Features

  • Four-Dimensional (4D) Pocket: A seemingly bottomless pouch on his belly that accesses a pocket dimension, holding thousands of high-tech "himitsu dōgu" (secret tools).
  • Power Controls: His red spherical tail functions as an on/off switch. Pulling it completely shuts him down.
  • Cat-Like Functions: Although a robot, his whiskers are meant to act as radars and his nose functions like a camera, though his internal systems are prone to malfunctioning.

💡 22nd-Century Fun Fact

Did you know? While Doraemon's outer shell is built from high-grade, recycled aerospace aluminum, his blue paint skin actually functions as an advanced, self-cleaning nano-coating. This specialized material naturally repels dirt and dust, which is why he always looks brand new—even after traveling through prehistoric times or dusty parallel dimensions!
